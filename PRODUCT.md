# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Health-conscious UK consumers, primarily women aged 22-40, already engaged in wellness routines (gym, clean eating, skincare). They're skeptical of supplement brands that over-promise and under-deliver. They read labels. They want science behind what they put in their body, but they also want something that tastes good and fits into their morning without friction. Pre-launch: they arrive from social discovery or word-of-mouth; the site's only job is to earn their email.

## Product Purpose

Flourish is a premium gut health drink mix taken before every meal. 7 clinically-studied ingredients in a precise ratio: ACV, ginger, inulin, cinnamon, turmeric, black pepper, lemon. Positioned against bloating, blood sugar spikes, and sluggish digestion. Pre-launch; site exists to build a waitlist and establish brand credibility before the product ships.

## Brand Personality

Grounded. Precise. Quietly confident.

Not a wellness brand that shouts. Not clinical or cold. The tone is that of a knowledgeable friend who happens to have done the research — assured without being preachy, warm without being cheesy. Ingredients are the story; the brand trusts them to do the talking.

## Anti-references

- Bloom Nutrition: pastel overload, influencer aesthetic, too loud
- Athletic Greens / AG1: corporate wellness, B2B feel, trust-theater
- Generic supplement DTC: hero-metric cards, gradient-on-everything, faux-scientific badges
- Generic Tailwind landing pages: cream backgrounds, identical card grids, Inter everywhere

## Design Principles

1. **Let the ingredients speak** — the formula is the differentiator; hero copy earns its place by pointing at the science, not restating the brand name
2. **Warm precision** — earthy palette reads as natural without sliding into rustic or apothecary clichés; always grounded in specific ingredient colors (turmeric, cinnamon, ACV)
3. **Trust through restraint** — fewer claims, better claims; whitespace is not emptiness, it's confidence
4. **Motion serves attention** — animations reveal hierarchy, not personality; the brand doesn't dance for the user
5. **Pre-launch honesty** — placeholders (packaging, article images) are acknowledged, not hidden; the brand is in-progress and the site can say so

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. `prefers-reduced-motion` respected on all animations. Color is never the sole carrier of meaning.
